Introduction

Al-Abbas Printers, a family business, was established in 1970 by Jawad Husayn Jafri (Late). The Jafri Brothers are working passionately to continue the legacy of their Late father Mr. Jawad Husayn Jafri, benefitting from the wisdom and expert knowledge passed down to them. For more than 5 decades the team at Al-Abbas has developed and modified many print treatments with innovative ideas and latest the technology. Al-Abbas has an in-house design studio which generates professional artworks and assists customers to achieve their inspiration in their product design. We are also pioneering in security printing i.e labelling, packaging, books and has the capabilities to create sophisticated and state of art products with international standards in the brand protection business. Al-Abbas have successfully combined the traditional craftmanship skills with latest digital technology. Al-Abbas is striving to fulfil the demands of the fast growing print industry with constant R&D and fresh ideas. Our dedicated team offers world class technology to Pakistani and Middle Eastern clients with the collaboration of leading European manufacturers.
